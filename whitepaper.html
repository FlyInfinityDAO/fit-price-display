1. Preface

Despite its remarkable growth, the cryptocurrency market continues to face fundamental structural challenges. The lack of transparency in asset backing, price manipulation by market whales, reliance on centralized order books, and the risks associated with speculative bubbles have left investors exposed to extreme and unpredictable volatility.

Fly Infinity Platform has been designed in response to these challenges. It is a decentralized platform with the goal of creating a transparent digital asset with real backing, built on a structure that reduces the risk of price reversal to zero.

2. Introduction to Fly Infinity Platform

Fly Infinity is a next-generation decentralized financial platform built upon two fully complementary economic components:

2.1 Fly Infinity Network

An intelligent and autonomous network responsible for injecting real capital into the token contract. This network continuously increases liquidity without the need to mint new tokens.

2.2 Fly Infinity Token (FIT)

The platform's primary digital asset, whose price is calculated not based on market hype, but solely on the ratio of DAI Reserve to Total Supply.

3. Pricing Formula (Bonding Curve)

The price of the FIT token is calculated transparently and immutably within the smart contract:

FIT Price = DAI Reserve ÷ Total Supply

Key Features of This Model:

  • Immutable: The price cannot be altered by any external entity.
  • Transparent: Calculable and verifiable at any moment.
  • Continuously Rising: Each purchase increases liquidity and raises the base price.

4. Unique Feature: Non-Diminishing Price

Fly Infinity is the only project where the base token price never decreases. This is achieved through the following logic:

Action Effect
Buy FIT Real DAI enters the contract → Liquidity increases → Price increases
Sell FIT Tokens are burned → Supply decreases → Price increases
Buy Fee (3%) Returns to liquidity → Price reinforcement
Sell Fee (6%) Returns to liquidity → Price reinforcement

Result: Even large sell-offs do not necessarily reduce the price — in many cases, they increase it.

Double Infinity Network Advantage

Unlock Superior Purchase Power Through Network Structure

The Double Infinity Network introduces a revolutionary mechanism that empowers users to purchase significantly larger amounts of FIT with substantially fewer ownership shares. This is achieved through the intelligent binary tree structure of the network, where each share placed in the network generates network points that directly increase the user's purchase capacity.

How the Binary Tree Multiplier Works:

  • Traditional Model: Each ownership share provides a fixed purchase cap of 100 DAI. To purchase 6,000 DAI worth of FIT, a user would need 60 shares, costing an additional 6,000 DAI — total investment of 12,000 DAI.
  • Double Infinity Model: By strategically placing shares in a balanced binary tree (1 upline + 2 downline per node), the same 6,000 DAI purchase can be achieved with only 40 shares — a 33% reduction in required shares.
  • Accumulated Advantage: As the purchase amount increases, the efficiency gap widens. For larger purchases, the Double Infinity Network can reduce required shares by up to 50% or more, dramatically lowering the total capital needed to acquire FIT.

Mathematical Proof: In a balanced binary tree with 40 shares, the network generates 57 reward points (as verified through our reward calculation model). Each point represents unlocked purchase capacity, allowing users to achieve higher purchase caps with fewer shares. This is not a promotional claim — it is a mathematically verifiable property of the Double Infinity Network architecture.

Key Takeaway: The Double Infinity Network is not merely a referral system — it is a financial engineering innovation that mathematically optimizes capital efficiency. Users who understand and leverage this structure can acquire FIT with significantly lower capital outlay compared to traditional direct purchase methods.
