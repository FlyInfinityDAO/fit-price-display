Formula:
Since the buy fee is 3%, the amount of DAI that actually buys FIT is:
DAI After Fee = DAI Paid × (1 - 0.03) = DAI Paid × 0.97
Then, Price per FIT = DAI After Fee ÷ FIT Received
Example: Pay 100 DAI, receive 15 FIT → DAI After Fee = 97 DAI → Price = 97 ÷ 15 = 6.466 DAI/FIT
