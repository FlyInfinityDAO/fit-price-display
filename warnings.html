⚠️ Before Changing or Deleting Your Wallet

If you intend to perform a wallet change operation, you must sell your tokens first. Otherwise, your tokens will lose their transferability and you will not be able to sell them afterward.

🔧 Solution:
First, sell all your FIT tokens → Then change your wallet → Finally, buy FIT again in your new wallet.
⚠️ Never Share Your Recovery Phrase

Recovery phrases (seed phrases) cannot be changed or reset. Keep them safe!

  • ✅ Write them down physically on paper
    ✅ Store multiple copies in secure, separate locations
    ✅ Never store them digitally (screenshots, cloud, email)
    ❌ Never share them with anyone, including "support" teams
⚠️ Blockchain Transactions Are Irreversible

Blockchain is a decentralized system with no central authority. Once a transaction is confirmed, it is permanently recorded on the blockchain and cannot be reversed, modified, or deleted by anyone.

  • 🔒 Always double-check wallet addresses before sending
    🔒 Verify transaction amounts carefully
    🔒 Be cautious of scams and phishing attempts
    🔒 No one can "reverse" or "cancel" your transaction
