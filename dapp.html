Agreement Road Map

📌 Preamble
By entering the Fly Infinity ecosystem, participants acknowledge their role as strategic investors within a decentralized financial framework. Acquisition of the native digital asset, FIT, is contingent upon the prior purchase of a Fly Infinity ownership share. Upon obtaining this share, participants are granted the right to acquire FIT tokens in varying amounts, ranging from $1 to $100 per transaction, either incrementally or in a single transaction, based on their discretion and investment strategy.

💎 Ownership Share – Lifetime, Non-Refundable
The capital contributed toward the acquisition of an ownership share constitutes a non-recoverable commitment. This contribution is not subject to withdrawal or refund under any circumstances. However, this share confers perpetual membership within the network, requiring no further renewal, top-up, or recurring fees throughout the participant's engagement with the ecosystem.

💰 FIT Digital Asset – Fully Liquid
In contrast to the ownership share, any FIT tokens acquired through the platform remain entirely liquid and redeemable at the participant's discretion. There are no restrictions on withdrawal, and participants may convert their FIT holdings to value at any time, without delay or penalty.

🎁 Gift Allocation – Lottery-Based Rewards
Each ownership share entitles the participant to a predetermined allocation of lottery-based rewards. Specifically, participants may win up to twenty (20) individual gifts, each valued at $5, through the platform's transparent and automated reward distribution mechanism.

🎁 Welcome Bonus – Instant Token Credit
Upon successful registration and activation of an ownership share, a welcome bonus equivalent to $2 in FIT tokens is instantly and automatically credited to the participant's wallet. This bonus serves as an introductory benefit and is processed at the exact moment of network entry.

🔗 Referral Identifier – Decentralized Invitation System
Each ownership share generates a unique and immutable referral identifier. This identifier is intrinsically linked to the participant's BNB wallet address, which functions as their official referral code. Through active community expansion and network development, participants are empowered to progressively unlock elevated purchase caps, enabling the acquisition of larger volumes of FIT tokens in subsequent transactions.

📖 For a comprehensive understanding of the tiered purchase structure and referral mechanics, please consult the official White Paper.

💰 Developer Rewards – Performance-Based Compensation
In recognition of network growth and community development, the ecosystem allocates monetary rewards in USD to qualifying participants. These rewards are processed and settled on an hourly basis, ensuring timely and transparent compensation for those contributing to the expansion of the Fly Infinity network.

🔒 Decentralized Infrastructure – Security & Anonymity
All transactions, interactions, and data exchanges within the Fly Infinity ecosystem are executed through a fully decentralized architecture. Participant assets, subscription records, and personal information are securely stored on the blockchain, ensuring the highest standards of security, transparency, and pseudonymity. No centralized entity has access to or control over user data or funds.

By continuing, you agree to the terms outlined above.
