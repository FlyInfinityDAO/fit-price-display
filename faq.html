Frequently Asked Questions

To purchase Fly Infinity ownership shares, you need to have the following in your wallet:

  • 100 DAI — for the ownership share purchase
  • Approximately $1 worth of BNB — for network gas fees

💡 Important: Make sure you have enough BNB in your wallet to cover transaction fees on the BNB Smart Chain network.

Once you have these amounts, follow the steps in the DApp to complete your ownership share purchase.

To purchase Fly Infinity ownership shares, follow these steps:

Step 1: Go to the DApp page.

Step 2: Connect your wallet (MetaMask, TrustWallet, or TokenPocket).

Step 3: First, approve 100 DAI for the network contract.

Step 4: Sign the Agreement Road Map.

Step 5: Enter your referrer address and complete registration as a network owner.

After registration, you will be recognized as an official owner of the Fly Infinity network and can enjoy all its benefits.

💡 Note: You must have at least 100 DAI in your wallet to purchase ownership shares.

To purchase FIT tokens, you must first buy Fly Infinity ownership shares, then follow these steps:

Step 1: After registering as an owner, return to the DApp page.

Step 2: Approve 1000 DAI for the token contract.

Step 3: Enter the amount of DAI you want to spend.

Step 4: Click the Buy FIT button and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

After confirmation, the equivalent FIT will be sent to your wallet.

💡 Important: You can only buy FIT after purchasing ownership shares.

Buy fee: 3%

To sell FIT tokens, follow these steps:

Step 1: Go to the DApp page.

Step 2: Connect your wallet.

Step 3: Enter the amount of FIT you want to sell.

Step 4: Click the Sell FIT button.

Step 5: Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

After confirmation, the equivalent DAI will be sent to your wallet.

💡 Note: Every FIT sale adds a portion of the fee to the DAI reserve, increasing the base token price.

Sell fee: 6%

No — FIT tokens cannot be transferred between wallets.

This restriction is designed for the following reasons:

  • ✅ Maintain the constantly rising (non-diminishing) price algorithm — Token transfers could create price volatility and a black market.
  • ✅ Prevent black market creation — All FIT trades must go through the smart contract to ensure transparency and security.
  • ✅ Full transparency — All transactions are visible and auditable on the blockchain.

💡 Conclusion: You can only trade FIT through the smart contract. FIT is non-transferable between wallets.

The only way to change ownership is to sell through the contract and have someone else buy.

Transaction fees in Fly Infinity Token are as follows:

  • Buying FIT: 3% of the input amount
  • Selling FIT: 6% of the output amount

These fees are automatically added to the DAI reserve, which increases the base token price.

💡 Key Point: The more transactions occur, the faster the FIT base price grows. This means every buy and sell increases the value of your tokens.

📊 All fees are transparent and viewable on the smart contract.

Unfortunately, if you lose your recovery phrase (Seed Phrase / Recovery Phrase), there is no way to recover your wallet.

The recovery phrase is the only way to access your wallet, and:

  • ❌ Cannot be changed
  • ❌ Cannot be reset
  • ❌ No support team can recover it
  • ❌ No one else has access to it

✅ Security recommendations:

  • Write your recovery phrase on paper and store it in a safe place.
  • Keep multiple copies in separate secure locations.
  • Never store it digitally (screenshots, cloud, email).
  • Never share it with anyone.

⚠️ Warning: No support team, including the Fly Infinity team, will ever ask for your recovery phrase. Beware of scammers!
